

# Sage-Combinat and Chevie workshop in Orsay

June 14-18 of 2010 

Purpose: strengthen the collaboration between Sage and Chevie, in particular around Coxeter groups and Hecke algebras 

Sponsored by the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay, Université Paris Sud ![combinat/SageCombinatChevieWorkshopOrsay2010/logo_UFR.png](combinat/SageCombinatChevieWorkshopOrsay2010/logo_UFR.png) 

<a class="http" href="http://trac.sagemath.org/sage_trac/wiki/SageCombinatChevieStatusReport">Status report</a> 


## Accommodation

Like for the previous <a class="http" href="http:/combinat/SageCombinatWorkshopOrsay">Sage-Combinat days of January 2009</a>, I (Nicolas) have rented a 8-person house in Cernay-la-Ville (<a class="http" href="http://www.cernayvacances.com/CFrans.html">la tour</a>). In case of overflow, we can setup tents in the adjacent garden. We will do the cooking ourselves (bring your favorite recipes and dishes!). Altogether this will make for a tight budget. 


## Program

The first day of the conference (Monday 14th) will take place in Orsay, bâtiment 425, and is open to anybody wanting to learn about <a class="http" href="http:www.sagemath.org/">Sage</a>. 

* 10am-11am, petit amphi: An introduction to Sage (Nicolas M. Thiéry) 
* 11am-1pm, (Salle Info Grand Amphi(20)), tutorials 
* 1pm-2pm, lunch 
* 2pm-5pm, tutorial

* From Chatelet-les-Halles: 45 minutes RER B to Saint-Remy les Chevreuses + 15 minutes bus to Cernay-la-Ville 
* Time tables Saint-Remy <-> Cernay <-> Rambouillet: 
      * <a class="http" href="http://www.savac.fr/fr/transports/horaires_content/horaire/25136_B_SAVAC_39_03_COMPLET.pdf">39.03</a> There are two bus stops at Cernay.
